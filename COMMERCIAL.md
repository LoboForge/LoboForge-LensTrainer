## How to request a license

Contact LoboForge:

- **GitHub:** open an issue or discussion on [github.com/LoboForge/LoboForge-LensTrainer](https://github.com/LoboForge/LoboForge-LensTrainer) with subject “Commercial license inquiry”, or
- **Email:** add your contact address here before publishing, or use GitHub private contact if enabled.

Include a short description of:

1. Your company or project name  
2. How you plan to use the trainer  
3. Whether use is internal, customer-facing, or redistributed  
4. Expected scale (users, seats, or revenue model)

We will respond with available options and pricing or revenue-share terms.
